About Me

I have a strong perfernce to all things light, bright and colourful.

Whether it be with sound, visuals, taste, or movemont, if there's some form of art occurring, I'll be there to admire.

The admiration instilled the curiosity to create.

My creative expressions have differed, changed and now grown to included the digital releam. The ability to access everyting there is online is the greatest gift of the modern era, and I intent to add the already vast expanse of knowlendge. For now, there is a gap in digital accessabilty to Indigenous knowlendge, I intent to help bridge that gap.
